For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. 3. Data Security and Integrity

We value your trust in providing us your personal information. For applications involving user accounts or sync features, we use Firebase Realtime Database to ensure your data is encrypted and stored using Google's world-class security protocols. Your passwords are never visible to us.

4. Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, and the time and date of your use of the Service.

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

7. Account and Data Deletion

In compliance with Google Play's Data Safety policies (specifically for Counter Tasbih Pro: Zikir App and RiseSocial: Profil Booster), we provide a straightforward way for users to request the deletion of their accounts and associated data. You can request data deletion even if you have already uninstalled the app.

  • How to Request: Send an email to any of our support addresses: ss.infowe@gmail.com, support-kaptech@googlegroups.com, or support-grow@googlegroups.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and provide your registered email address.
  • Data to be Deleted: Upon request, we will permanently delete your user profile, authentication records, and all application-specific data collected by Counter Tasbih Pro or RiseSocial stored in our Firebase database.
  • Retention Period: Once the request is verified, data deletion will be processed within 30 days. Some non-identifiable log data may be retained temporarily for security and debugging purposes as permitted by law.
  • Verification & Re-Registration: To verify if your deletion request has been completed, you may attempt to access the Login menu in the respective app. If the account has been successfully deleted, the system will no longer recognize your previous data and will treat the attempt as a new registration.
